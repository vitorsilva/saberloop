Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 12, 2025

Saberloop ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Summary

Your data stays on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal information to our servers.

Data Storage

Saberloop stores data locally on your device using your browser's IndexedDB. This includes:

  • Quiz history and scores
  • App settings and preferences
  • Your OpenRouter API key (if you choose to provide one)

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly connect to an AI provider.

AI Provider Connection

When you generate quiz questions, the app connects to OpenRouter (a third-party AI API provider). In this case:

  • Your quiz topic and preferences are sent to generate questions
  • Your API key is sent for authentication
  • No personal identifying information is sent

OpenRouter has its own privacy policy governing how they handle requests.

In the future, we may integrate additional AI providers. If these providers require different information, we will update this policy accordingly and notify users in advance.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal information (name, email, etc.)
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Device identifiers
  • Location data
  • Cookies for tracking purposes

In the future, we may collect anonymized, non-personally-identifiable information such as aggregated quiz statistics, performance metrics, or general usage patterns. This data would not be traceable to individual users. Any such changes will be communicated in advance.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by:

  • Clearing your browser's site data for saberloop.com
  • Uninstalling the app (if installed as PWA)

Children's Privacy

Saberloop does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. The app can be used by users of all ages as no personal data is collected.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be communicated in advance through the app or our website, and will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
