Why it's Canberra

While Sydney is the most populous city, Canberra was chosen as the capital in 1908 as a compromise between rivals Sydney and Melbourne.

It is an entirely planned city, designed specifically to serve as the seat of government, following an international design contest won by American architects Walter Burley Griffin and Marion Mahony Griffin.
